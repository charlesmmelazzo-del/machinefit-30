Evidence Rules Used

  • Train major muscle groups at least 2 days per week, with daily movement when recovery is good.
  • Use 2-3 work sets for most movements, with 6-12 reps for muscle, 8-12 reps for general strength, and short circuits for conditioning.
  • Increase machine load 2-10% when you beat the rep target by 1-2 reps for two exposures with clean form.
  • Keep 1-3 reps in reserve on most sets; save true failure for rare final sets.
  • Every fourth week trims volume so your joints can catch up while your streak continues.
